Habitat

Last Updated 09/28/17 by Anonymous Ferret


  Ferrets live in many different places around the globe, but the species of ferret does vary according to location. The European Ferret is found throughout Europe as well as northern and western Asia and some parts of North Africa. The black-footed ferret is found in North America. Ferrets can live in a variety of habitats, including plains, forests, mountainous regions, deserts, tundra and grasslands. However, their natural habitat is being destroyed and so the number of ferrets in the wild is decreasing.
